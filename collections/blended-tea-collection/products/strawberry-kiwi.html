Strawberry Kiwi

Imperial Tea Garden

$4.00

Packaging Options
  • Description
  • Grade
  • Brewing
  • Caffeine
  • Strawberry Kiwi is a blend of dried herbs and fruit from Canada, United States, Thailand, and Spain.  This herb and fruit beverage has a down under appeal  with natural tartness that offers a bold and refreshing flavor.  Sweeten with sugar to bring out an even bolder flavor. 



  • Origin:  Ontario Canada / California USA / Sing Buri Thailand / Seville Spain

    Grade:  Grade #1 Herb & Fruit Pieces

    Altitude: Various

    Manufacture Type:  Blended.  Blend of air, sun and machine dried herbs and fruits

    Characteristics:  Bold strawberry kiwi flavor, tart, full bodied



  • Hot Tea Brewing Recommendations:
    AMOUNT TO USE: Put 2 slightly heaping teaspoons of loose tea for each 7-9 oz/200-260 ml of water in the teapot. Pour freshly boiled water over tea.

    BREWING RECOMMENDATIONS: Steep 5-10 minutes. Garnish and sweeten to taste.


    Iced Tea Preparation Recommendations:
    AMOUNT TO USE: To make 1 L/1 QT, put 12 slightly heaping teaspoons of loose tea into a teapot. Pour 1¼ cups/315ml of boiling water over tea.

    BREWING RECOMMENDATIONS: Steep 5-10 minutes. Fill a serving pitcher 1/4 full with cold water. Pour tea into serving pitcher.

  • Caffeine Level ~ NONE - Naturally Caffeine Free

     

Collections: Blended Tea Collection, Herbal Tea Collection

Category: Blended Tea Collection, Herbal Tea Collection

Type: Herbal Tea
